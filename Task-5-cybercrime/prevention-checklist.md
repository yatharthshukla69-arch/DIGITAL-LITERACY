## Stay Safe Online – Practical Cybercrime Prevention Checklist  
**Prepared by:** Yatharth Shukla | 25BAI10751 

---

###  Actionable Safety Tips for College Students

1. Always verify the identity of a person before responding to payment requests, job offers, or unknown messages.  
2. Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — it is only required for sending payments.  
3. Do not approve collect requests from unknown individuals on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.  
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links related to internships, scholarships, or job opportunities without verification.  
5. Cross-check payment confirmations through trusted sources instead of relying on screenshots.  
6. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, banking, and social media accounts.  
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi networks while making financial transactions or logging into sensitive accounts.  
8. Regularly monitor your bank transactions and report any unusual activity immediately.  
9. Install apps only from official sources and keep your device updated to prevent security vulnerabilities.  
10. Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details even if the caller claims to be from a bank or authority.  

---

###  Financial / UPI Safety Focus

- Always check whether a request is **“Pay” or “Receive”** before entering your UPI PIN.  
- Never scan QR codes sent by unknown people for receiving money — it may lead to debit instead.

###  Reporting Cybercrime (India)

If you become a victim of cyber fraud, act immediately:

-  National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
-  Helpline Number: **1930**  

---

###  Final Reminder

Think before you click, verify before you trust, and act only after understanding the transaction. Awareness is the most effective defense against cybercrime.
Cybersecurity is not just a technical responsibility but a **behavioral discipline**.  
In UPI fraud cases, the user unknowingly authorizes the transaction — making awareness, patience, and verification the most powerful defense mechanisms.
